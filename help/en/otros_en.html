Other Considerations

  • Comments may be written after an instruction or on separate lines by using double slash ( // ). Everything after // up to the end of the line is ignored when the algorithm is interpreted. Using /* and */ for comments is not valid.
  • There cannot be instructions outside the process (before PROCESO or after FINPROCESO), although comments are allowed there.
  • Non-sequential structures may be nested. In other words, they may contain others inside them, but the contained structure must begin and end within the containing one.
  • Identifiers, or variable names, must consist only of letters, numbers, and/or underscore (_), always beginning with a letter.
  • Variable data types are not declared explicitly, but inferred from their use.
  • Character constants are written between quotation marks ( " ).
  • In numeric constants, the dot ( . ) is the decimal separator.
  • Logical constants are Verdadero and Falso.
  • This pseudo-language now supports the creation of new functions or subprocesses in the corresponding profiles; see SubProcesses.
