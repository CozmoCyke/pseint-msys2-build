Notes about PSeInt (https://pseint.sourceforge.net)

PSeInt is a tool designed to assist students in their first steps in programming. Through a simple and intuitive pseudo-language in Spanish (complemented by a flowchart editor), it lets them focus on the fundamental concepts of computational algorithmics, minimizing the difficulties of a real language while providing a work environment with many aids and learning resources.

To see what is new in each PSeInt update, open the Change Log.

To contact the author (to report bugs, request improvements, leave comments or suggestions, etc.), follow this link.
