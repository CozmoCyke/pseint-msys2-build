Cucarachas Racing (blog)

    Cucarachas Racing is the name of a blog written by the developer of PSeInt. There you can find articles about the history and management of the project, technical discussions about how some features were implemented and evolved, reflections on the purpose of using a pseudo-language to teach programming, future plans, responses to common requests, and much more. You can access through this link the subset of articles directly related to PSeInt.
